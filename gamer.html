As A Gamer

Gaming History

For a time I was addicted to Video games, or I would rather put it as escaping from the real world into the digital world. I even wrote a whole seven-page essay about it (Check it out in my e-portfolio if interested) . Now that I have thought about it more, my opinion is once again a little different. But hey, who has the right to judge such things. Video games, just like everything else, has its upsides and downsides. It's up to the players. There are Gourmets and there are gluttons. I, as a player and a semi-professional developer view games as simply products, just like other softwares.
